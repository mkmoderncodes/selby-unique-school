Admissions

We welcome applications for all levels. Start your child's journey with Selby Unique School today.

How to Enrol

Admissions Are Open

We accept applications year-round, with the main intake at the start of each academic year in September. However, we also consider mid-term enrolments depending on availability of space.


Our admissions process is simple, transparent, and designed to be easy for every Ghanaian family. Simply fill out the online form below or visit us in person during school hours.

1

Birth Certificate

A certified copy of your child's Ghana birth certificate is required for all levels.

2

Passport Photos

Two recent passport-sized photographs of the child applying for admission.

3

Previous School Report

Most recent school report card (for Primary and JHS applicants transferring in).

4

Parent / Guardian ID

A valid Ghana Card or other national ID of the parent or guardian enrolling the child.

5

Health Records

Child's immunisation and health records, especially for Crèche and KG applicants.

6

Application Fee

A non-refundable application processing fee is required at the time of submission.

Common Questions

Admissions FAQs

Have questions? Find quick answers to the most common admissions queries below.

The main academic year at Selby Unique School begins in September, in line with the Ghana Education Service (GES) academic calendar. However, we also accept mid-term enrolments throughout the year, subject to class availability.
For Crèche and KG levels, there is no entrance test — we welcome all children within the appropriate age range. For Primary and JHS transfers, we conduct a brief informal assessment to help us place students at the most appropriate level for their learning needs.
School fees vary by level and term. Please contact our admissions office directly for the current fee structure. We are happy to discuss payment plans and available options. You can reach us via phone or email using the details on our Contact page.
Please contact our school office to ask about available transport arrangements in your area. We will do our best to connect you with the right information or options.
Absolutely! We warmly encourage parents and guardians to visit Selby Unique School before making their decision. You can schedule a visit by calling us or sending an email. Our team will give you a guided tour and answer all your questions.
Selby Unique School has an official school uniform. Full details, including colours and where to obtain the uniform, will be provided at the time of admission. Our admissions team can also answer this during your visit or via phone.

Still Have Questions?

Our admissions team is ready to help. Reach out to us by phone, email, or visit us in person during school hours.
