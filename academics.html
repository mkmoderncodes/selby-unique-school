Our Academics

GES-aligned education from early childhood through to Junior High School — every level, every child.

Our Curriculum

Ghana Education Service (GES) Curriculum

Selby Unique School follows the official Ghana Education Service curriculum, ensuring all students receive a nationally recognised, high-quality education that prepares them for life beyond the classroom.

🇬🇭

GES Approved

All subjects, syllabi, and assessments follow the Ghana Education Service standards and guidelines for each level.

📊

Regular Assessment

Students are assessed regularly through class tests, end-of-term exams, and continuous assessment to track progress.

🎯

BECE Preparation

Our JHS students receive dedicated preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) with strong results.

🍼

Crèche

Ages 0 – 2 years

Our Crèche provides a warm, safe, and stimulating environment for Ghana's youngest learners. Trained caregivers offer nurturing, responsive care that supports each infant's physical, cognitive, and emotional development.


We follow routines that provide comfort and security while introducing gentle sensory play, language exposure, and early social experiences. Parents can rest assured knowing their little ones are in caring, professional hands.


Key Development Areas
Sensory Play Motor Skills Language Exposure Social Bonding Music & Rhythm Story Time Physical Development Emotional Security

What We Offer

  • Fully trained and caring nursery staff
  • Safe, clean, and stimulating play spaces
  • Nutritious snacks and meal support
  • Daily parent updates and communication
  • Age-appropriate sensory activities
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Structured daily routines for comfort
🌱

Kindergarten (KG)

Ages 3 – 5 years (KG 1 & KG 2)

Kindergarten at Selby Unique School follows the GES Early Childhood Education (ECE) framework. Children engage in playful, structured learning that builds early literacy, numeracy, and social skills essential for Primary school success.


Our KG classrooms are vibrant, colourful, and filled with engaging learning materials. We use a mix of play-based learning, guided activities, and creative projects to make every day exciting and educational.


GES KG Subjects
Language & Literacy Numeracy Creative Arts Our World & Environment Physical Education Religious & Moral Ed. Music & Movement Ghanaian Language

What Your Child Will Learn

  • Letter recognition, sounds, and early reading
  • Numbers, counting, and basic shapes
  • Colours, patterns, and creative expression
  • How to interact with peers and share
  • Basic health, hygiene, and safety habits
  • Songs, rhymes, and Ghanaian language basics
  • Following instructions and routines
📚

Primary School

Ages 6 – 11 years (Primary 1 – 6)

Primary school at Selby Unique School covers Primary 1 through Primary 6, following the GES curriculum for each year. This is where foundational academic skills are built, expanded, and deepened through engaging teaching methods.


Our teachers use a combination of interactive lessons, group work, project-based learning, and regular assessments to ensure every pupil is progressing well and enjoying their education journey.


GES Primary Subjects
English Language Mathematics Science Social Studies Ghanaian Language Religious & Moral Ed. Creative Arts Physical Education French Computing / ICT

Primary Highlights

  • Strong focus on English literacy and comprehension
  • Problem-solving and mental maths training
  • Science experiments and hands-on learning
  • Ghanaian cultural heritage integration
  • ICT and basic computer skills
  • End-of-term examinations and reporting
  • Sports and extracurricular activities
🏅
End of Primary
Promotion to JHS
🎓

Junior High School (JHS)

Ages 12 – 15 years (JHS 1 – 3)

Junior High School is the final stage of basic education in Ghana. At Selby Unique School, JHS students receive rigorous academic training, exam preparation, and personal development support to prepare them for secondary education and beyond.


Our JHS programme culminates in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), which determines placement into Senior High School. We have a proven track record of excellent BECE results thanks to our dedicated teachers and structured revision programmes.


GES JHS Core Subjects
English Language Mathematics Integrated Science Social Studies Religious & Moral Ed. Ghanaian Language Computing / ICT French Career Technology Physical Education Creative Arts

JHS Programme Features

  • Comprehensive BECE exam preparation
  • Past questions and mock examinations
  • Subject-specialist teachers for each course
  • Career guidance and SHS placement support
  • Leadership and prefectship opportunities
  • Science practicals and lab activities
  • Extra classes for BECE candidates
🎓
End of JHS 3
BECE Examination
→ Senior High School Placement
School Year

Academic Calendar

Our academic year follows the Ghana Education Service (GES) term structure, divided into three terms.

📅

First Term

September – December

School resumes in September. New students are welcomed, and the academic year begins with the GES curriculum for all levels. Term ends with end-of-term examinations before the Christmas break.

📅

Second Term

January – April

The second term continues from January. Mid-term assessments and end-of-term exams are conducted. JHS 3 students begin intensive BECE preparation programmes this term.

📅

Third Term

May – August

The final term includes year-end assessments, cultural events, graduation ceremonies, and the BECE for JHS 3 students. Promotions and new admissions also take place during this period.
