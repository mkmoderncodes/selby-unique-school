About Selby Unique School

Discover our story, our purpose, and our commitment to quality education in Ghana.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Everything we do at Selby Unique School is guided by a clear purpose: to raise well-rounded, educated young Ghanaians.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, inclusive, and holistic education aligned with the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum, equipping every student with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in life and contribute meaningfully to Ghanaian society.


We are committed to creating a nurturing, disciplined, and stimulating learning environment where every child — from Crèche to Junior High School — is valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the leading school of choice in Ghana, recognised for academic excellence, strong moral values, and the production of responsible, innovative, and globally competitive graduates who are proud of their Ghanaian heritage.


We envision a future where every student who passes through Selby Unique School becomes a beacon of integrity, intellectual achievement, and positive change in Ghana and the world.

School Overview

Our Story

Selby Unique School was founded with one powerful belief: that every child in Ghana deserves access to excellent education. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted educational institution serving hundreds of families.


Located in Ghana, our school offers a complete educational journey from the earliest years of childhood through to Junior High School, all under one roof. We follow the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum to ensure our students are fully prepared for national examinations and lifelong learning.


Our motto — "Christ Our Redeemer" — reflects our foundational belief in hope, purpose, and the potential within every child, guiding our values of discipline, respect, and excellence.
