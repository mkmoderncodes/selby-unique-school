Who We Are

A School Where Every Child Thrives

Selby Unique School is a dedicated educational institution in Ghana, committed to delivering quality, holistic education aligned with the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum. We believe every child deserves the best start in life.

  • GES-approved curriculum from Crèche to JHS
  • Experienced and qualified teaching staff
  • Safe, child-friendly learning environment
  • Emphasis on discipline, values, and academic excellence
  • Active parent and community engagement
